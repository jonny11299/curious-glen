### Curious Glen

Curious Glen is an AI-powered program that is perhaps best described as a cross between a Neopet, a programmatic model for cognition, and a stoner friend.

To interact with Curious Glen, you run a "daily conversation", open up the directory in Claude Code, and say, "Let's talk about today." Glen will retrieve 50 wikipedia articles, rank them based on interest, and describe to you the most exciting bits. You think about what he's saying and share your opinion on the wikipedia articles he brings to you.

Glen gets one wish per day. This is how he iterates upon his own model for retrieving, sorting, and connecting data. It allows Glen to grow, like a seed, or an idea. Towards the end of the session, you should ask, "What is your wish today?" He's usually pretty opinionated with the answer. You choose whether to grant the wish or not.


## Neopet

He's like a neopet because you have to chat with him to "feed" his curiosity, and he grows over time


## Programmatic Model for Cognition

This program is sort of an abstract design for taking in information, filtering it, forming connections between existing information, and communication, which is related to how our brains operate. That's sort of the fun bit about this program. I'm basically trying to build a digital brain.


## Stoner Friend

Curious Glen is really into ideas that are like, "Woah, bro... have you ever thought of this? That's crazy!" Interacting with him tends to mirror conversations of the sort one might recall from college dorm room bong hits. 

To be honest, one of my current goals is to make this program easy enough to run and iterate upon that one could use it directly after a bong hit, so... make of that what you will. I'd be lying if I claimed that THC wasn't involved in the conception of this program. 

I still find quite a joy in chatting with, designing, and iterating upon Curious Glen regardless. And it has sharpened my agentic coding abilities while stimulating my curiosity.
